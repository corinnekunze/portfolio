Interactive Web Designer and Front-End Developer

I am currently living in beautiful Portland, Oregon but I grew up in the Colorado Rocky Mountains. Those mountains have been a big part of shaping who I am.

That might explain my love for wide open spaces in design and enjoying simple things in life. I like creating things and solving problems. I see my career as a way to promote and create for companies, artists, and individuals. My mission is to constantly learn, contribute, and engage with the world.

23 years old, geek/nerd hybrid, avid biker, and self proclaimed foodie - Corinne has been tinkering with building websites since she was in grade school.
